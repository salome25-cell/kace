the birth of kace jewelry

kace jewelry was created on the 17th of April, 2026, by Salome Lwanzo with the invaluable collaboration of Michelle Javaid. What began as a spark — an advertisement that caught Salome's eye — evolved into a bold, beautiful movement. Inspired by the interplay of light and lace texture, Salome decided to make a new move and craft a brand where artistry meets durability.

Every piece of kace jewelry is designed for those who see fashion as an extension of personality. From delicate chainmail-like lace motifs to soft metallic weaves, the brand redefines jewelry as second skin. Our mission is to fuse craftsmanship with modern vision — because beauty should never be fragile, but resilient.
